Server Rules - ShadowFaction SMP

1. General Conduct

  • Show respect to all players and staff members.
  • Do not impersonate staff or other players.
  • Maintain a friendly atmosphere — even in competitive environments.
  • Toxicity and excessive salt will not be tolerated.

2. Raiding & Stealing

  • Raiding and stealing are allowed as part of faction warfare.
  • You may loot enemy bases and engage in PvP — be strategic, not abusive.
  • Spawn camping or repeated harassment outside of conflict is not allowed.

3. Client Modifications & Fair Play

  • No hacked clients, auto-clickers, or unfair advantages.
  • Allowed mods: OptiFine, minimaps (no radar), performance enhancers, shader packs.
  • Using exploits or glitches will result in punishment.

4. Chat Rules

  • Keep chat civil and appropriate for all players.
  • No hate speech, racism, or targeted harassment.
  • Friendly trash talk is fine — keep it fun, not toxic.
  • No spamming, flooding, or advertising.

5. Staff & Reports

  • Listen to staff at all times — their word is final.
  • Abuse towards staff will result in punishment.
  • Found a bug or rule breaker? Report it directly — your help matters.

6. PvP Guidelines

  • PvP is always enabled — consent is not required.
  • Use good sportsmanship — avoid excessive spawn camping.
  • Trap setups are allowed but must not abuse spawn zones.

7. Exploits & Bugs

  • Do not use unintended game mechanics for advantage (e.g., duplication).
  • Report bugs and exploits immediately — honesty is rewarded.

8. Community Standards

  • Do not attempt to crash, lag, or disrupt the server intentionally.
  • Do not share personal information — yours or others’.
  • Have fun and help build a great faction-based community!
